Rajalakshmi Engineering College has established an impressive placement record, demonstrating its commitment to ensuring that students transition successfully from academia to industry. The college’s robust placement cell actively collaborates with a wide range of leading national and international companies to facilitate internships and full-time job opportunities for its graduates. Over the years, REC has consistently achieved high placement rates, with a significant percentage of students securing offers from top-tier companies across various sectors including technology, engineering, finance, and management. The placement cell organizes numerous pre-placement activities such as mock interviews, resume workshops, and industry guest lectures to prepare students for the competitive job market. Additionally, the college’s strong network of alumni, who are well-placed in prestigious organizations worldwide, also contributes to the placement success by offering mentorship and networking opportunities. REC’s emphasis on industry-oriented education, coupled with its state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, ensures that students are equipped with the skills and knowledge required to excel in their careers. The placement record is a testament to the college's dedication to student success and its ability to adapt to the evolving demands of the job market.
