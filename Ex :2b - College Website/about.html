Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC), located in Chennai, India, is a premier institution renowned for its excellence in engineering education. Established in 1997, REC is affiliated with Anna University and is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. REC is committed to fostering a conducive learning environment with state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and robust industry collaborations. The institution places a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and the overall development of its students, ensuring they are well-prepared for global challenges. With a vibrant campus life, comprehensive placement support, and numerous extracurricular activities, REC strives to create a holistic educational experience. The college's mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and ethical values to become successful professionals and responsible citizens.
