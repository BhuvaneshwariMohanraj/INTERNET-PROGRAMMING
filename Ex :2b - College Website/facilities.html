Rajalakshmi Engineering College offers comprehensive transportation services and well-equipped hostel facilities to ensure a comfortable and convenient experience for our students.

Transportation Facilities:

Our extensive bus network includes over 110 buses that cover four districts, providing reliable and efficient transportation for students and staff. The buses are well-maintained and equipped with modern amenities to ensure safety and comfort.

Hostel Facilities:

The college provides comfortable and well-furnished hostel accommodations for both male and female students. Hostels feature spacious rooms, comfortable beds, and study areas. On-campus dining services offer a variety of nutritious meals, and recreational facilities include indoor games, common rooms with TV, and study lounges.
