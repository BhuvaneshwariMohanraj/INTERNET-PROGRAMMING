About REC

Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC), located in Chennai, India, is a premier institution renowned for its excellence in engineering education. Established in 1997, REC is affiliated with Anna University and is accredited by the NBA and NAAC. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines.

REC is committed to fostering a conducive learning environment with state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and robust industry collaborations. The institution places a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and the overall development of its students, ensuring they are well-prepared for global challenges.
